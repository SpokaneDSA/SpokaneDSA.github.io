# Contact Spokane DSA

<!-- [Image placeholder: Spokane DSA logo or members welcoming new people] -->

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you want to join, have questions, or want to work together on a project, please reach out.

## Get in Touch

### Email
**spokanedsa@gmail.com**

Send us an email for:
- Questions about joining or membership
- Information about upcoming meetings
- Ideas for campaigns or actions
- Media inquiries
- Collaboration with your organization

We check our email regularly and will get back to you soon!

### Social Media

Follow us and send us a message on:

- **Facebook**: [facebook.com/SpokaneDSA](https://www.facebook.com/SpokaneDSA)
- **Instagram**: [@spokanedsa](https://www.instagram.com/spokanedsa)
- **GitHub**: [github.com/SpokaneDSA](https://github.com/SpokaneDSA)

We post updates about events, actions, and important issues on our social media. Follow us to stay connected!

### Come to a Meeting

<!-- [Image placeholder: People at a welcoming meeting] -->

The best way to get to know us is to come to a meeting!

**Monthly General Meeting**  
First Tuesday of every month from 5:30 PM to 7:00 PM

Everyone is welcome, whether you're already a member or just curious about what we do. Email us at spokanedsa@gmail.com for the meeting link. After the meeting, we usually head to a local pub or restaurant to socialize.

## Questions?

### For New Members

- **"How do I join?"** - Check out our [Join page](/join/) for all the details
- **"Do I need to be a member to come to a meeting?"** - No! Anyone can attend our meetings
- **"What happens at meetings?"** - We discuss issues, plan actions, and make decisions together
- **"I'm new to activism, is that okay?"** - Absolutely! We welcome people at all experience levels

### For Current Members

- **Need meeting information?** - Email spokanedsa@gmail.com
- **Want to start a new project?** - Bring your idea to the monthly meeting
- **Have a question about membership?** - Reach out via email or at a meeting

### For Organizations

Other groups interested in working with us on campaigns or events should email us at spokanedsa@gmail.com. We love working with other organizations to make Spokane better!

### For Media

If you're a reporter or journalist, please email us at spokanedsa@gmail.com with:
- Your name and news organization
- What you're writing about
- Your deadline

## Our Location

<!-- [Image placeholder: Map of Spokane or local landmark] -->

We're based in Spokane, Washington, and we serve the greater Spokane area and surrounding communities in the Inland Northwest.

## Office Hours

We're all volunteers, so we don't have a physical office or set office hours. The best ways to reach us are:
- Email us at spokanedsa@gmail.com
- Message us on social media
- Come to our monthly meeting (first Tuesday from 5:30 PM to 7:00 PM)

## National DSA

For questions about DSA nationally, visit [dsausa.org](https://dsausa.org) or email info@dsausa.org.

For questions about our local Spokane chapter, email spokanedsa@gmail.com.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you share my contact information?**  
A: No. We keep your information private and only use it to communicate with you about DSA activities.

**Q: How quickly will you respond?**  
A: We're all volunteers, but we try to respond within a few days. If it's time-sensitive, please say so in your message.

**Q: Can I remain anonymous?**  
A: You can ask questions anonymously, but to join or participate you'll need to provide contact information.
