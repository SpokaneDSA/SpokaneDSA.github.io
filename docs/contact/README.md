# Contact Spokane DSA

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you're interested in joining, have questions about our work, or want to collaborate on an action or campaign, please reach out.

## Get in Touch

### Email
For general inquiries, please contact us via the national DSA website and specify the Spokane chapter.

### Social Media
Follow us and send us a message:
- **GitHub**: [github.com/SpokaneDSA](https://github.com/SpokaneDSA)

### In Person
Come to one of our monthly general meetings! Check our [Events page](/events/) for meeting times and locations.

## Questions?

### For New Members
If you're interested in joining DSA or learning more about our chapter, check out our [Join page](/join/) or come to a general meeting.

### For Press
Members of the media should indicate their affiliation and the nature of their inquiry.

### For Collaboration
Other organizations and groups interested in working with Spokane DSA on campaigns or actions are encouraged to reach out.

## Location

We're based in Spokane, Washington, serving the greater Spokane area and surrounding communities.

## National DSA

For general questions about DSA nationally, visit [dsausa.org](https://dsausa.org) or email info@dsausa.org.
